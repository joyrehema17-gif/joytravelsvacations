About Joy Travels & Vacations

Your trusted safari partner in Kenya

Who We Are

Joy Travels and Vacations is a Kenyan-based travel company dedicated to providing unforgettable safari and beach experiences. We specialize in customized tours across Kenya’s most beautiful destinations including the Maasai Mara, Amboseli, Tsavo, Lake Nakuru, and the coastal regions like Watamu and Diani.

Meet the Director

Joy Rehema

Joy Rehema is the founder and lead tour guide of Joy Travels and Vacations. With a passion for tourism and years of experience in guiding safaris, she ensures every client enjoys a safe, exciting, and memorable journey.

Her deep knowledge of Kenya’s wildlife, culture, and hidden gems allows her to create unique travel experiences tailored to each guest.
